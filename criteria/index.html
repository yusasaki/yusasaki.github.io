III. 認定ラインと合格基準

  • FDE Professional 認定ライン＝全9領域で Lv3「できた」B評定以上。
  • 合格基準＝必須スキル項目100%＋選択スキル項目60%以上の充足（領域別判定）。
  • スキルカバレッジ%＝保有スキル項目数 ÷ 等級別要件項目数 ×100。カバレッジ%は表示指標であり、合否判定値ではありません。

This page defines the public criteria (invariant layer) of the FDE (Forward Deployed Engineer) certification: the competency domains, evaluation levels, certification line, and coverage metric. Individual test items, scoring anchors, and field-assessment assignments are not published, to preserve assessment integrity.

I. 9 COMPETENCY DOMAINS

#Domain#Domain#Domain
D1Data Measurement & Impact ValidationD4DeliveryD7Communication
D2Security & GovernanceD5Technical ImplementationD8Deal & Value Design
D3Organizational Collaboration & Knowledge TransferD6Business UnderstandingD9System Design & Integration

II. 5-LEVEL EVALUATION

LevelNameEvidencePlain-words anchor
Lv1ReactionConceptual understanding"I understand"
Lv2LearningTests & skill demonstration"I can do it"
Lv3BehaviorApplication in the field (two independent assessors)"I did it" ★Certification line
Lv4ResultsMeasurable outcomes"I changed it"
Lv5ROIMonetized outcomes ÷ investment"I proved it"

III. CERTIFICATION LINE & PASSING RULE

  • FDE Professional certification line = Grade B or above at Lv3 ("I did it") across all 9 domains.
  • Passing rule = 100% of required skill items plus at least 60% of elective skill items, judged per domain.
  • Skill Coverage % = held skill items ÷ tier requirement items × 100. Coverage % is a display metric, not a pass/fail value.

IV. 4 CERTIFICATION TIERS

TierRequirement (summary)
FDE AssociateLv1–2 attainment
FDE ProfessionalLv3, Grade B or above across all 9 domains ★Practice-certified
FDE ExpertLv4–5 impact track record
FDE FellowPost-Expert contribution to developing the next generation

The Foundations Certificate of Completion is a separate category of completion document and is not a digital certification badge.

V. VERIFICATION

Badges under this standard are issued in conformance with the Open Badges specification. Each badge's Evidence URL links to a Report Card hosted on this domain, allowing any third party to verify the underlying grades at any time. The Issuer is fixed as a single entity: ART & SCIENCE / Global FDE Standard.
